Suggested homepage flow

What visitors should feel

The homepage should make people feel like they have stepped into a clean, elegant artist world rather than a generic resume site. The first visual says presence. The copy says range. The page links say there is more to discover.

You can later add a soft autoplay showreel teaser, a press quote strip, or a featured news section for screenings, readings, or new projects.

Creative ideas to add later

Things that could make it even more distinct

Consider a rotating quote from your poems on the homepage, a “currently making” section, a visual mood-board page, or a featured journal note that changes monthly. Those touches make the site feel authored, not templated.
